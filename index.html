Hi. I'm Aaron.

I am a strategist, creator, and innovator specializing in content development, digital engagement, and graphic design. My expertise includes graphic design, videography, photography, and multimedia content for print, web, and social media. With advanced skills in Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS, SQL, and data analysis, I deliver projects that combine creative vision with data-driven results – always aiming to exceed expectations for engagement and brand impact.

Currently, as the Slate CRM Administrator for Purdue’s Graduate Student and Postdoctoral Scholars Office, I manage visual and technical updates, integrate enterprise systems, and streamline digital processes. My academic background in business administration and instructional design gives me a strong foundation in both creative and analytical thinking. I thrive in fast-paced environments, juggling multiple projects while producing innovative, high-impact visual content that connects with diverse audiences.
