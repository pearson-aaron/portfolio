Hi. I'm Aaron.

I am a strategist, creator, and innovator with a proven track record in content development, digital engagement, and graphic design. My expertise spans videography, post-production editing, photography, PDF design, graphic design, data analysis, HTML, CSS, SQL, and advanced spreadsheeting. I thrive in fast-paced, collaborative environments and lead projects from concept through execution with an Agile, user-focused approach. My work is grounded in data-driven decision-making and creative problem-solving, ensuring every deliverable not only meets but exceeds expectations for engagement and brand impact. I bring a keen eye for detail, a strong sense of organization, and a passion for producing innovative visual content that resonates across digital platforms, including social media and web.

Currently, as the Slate CRM Administrator for Purdue’s Graduate Students and Postdoctoral Scholars Office, I am responsible for implementing both visual and technical updates to digital platforms, integrating data with enterprise systems like Salesforce and CAS, and continuously streamlining processes for greater efficiency. I design and deliver comprehensive training materials, including videos and interactive modules, and lead instructional sessions for diverse audiences. My academic background includes an associate and bachelor’s degree in business administration and a master’s in instructional design and technology, equipping me with both the creative and analytical skills essential for high-impact graphic content production. My experience at Purdue has honed my ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, adapt quickly to changing priorities, and communicate ideas with clarity and creativity.
